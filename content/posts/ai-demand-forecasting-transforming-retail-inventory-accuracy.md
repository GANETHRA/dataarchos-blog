### Machine Learning Models at the Core

At the heart of **AI demand forecasting** are advanced ML models like LSTM (Long Short-Term Memory) networks and gradient boosting machines. These tools excel at time-series analysis, capturing non-linear patterns that traditional methods miss.

For instance, in inventory optimization, Data Archos uses ensemble models to predict demand at the SKU level across thousands of stores. This granularity allows for hyper-local forecasting—urban vs. rural demands can vary wildly, and AI captures it seamlessly.
